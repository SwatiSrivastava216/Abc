Description

The game involves an online treasure hunt where the participants will have to hunt for the answers and clues in order to reach to the end, thus unveiling the treasure. Participation can be done individually or in teams consisting of 2-4 members. Each clue will lead to another and can be solved by using the knowledge of the world of business. In order to move further the individual/teams will have to answer the questions as they are displayed. Each question will have equal points and the progress of each participant will be traced by our leaderboard. The participant gaining highest points by completing the trivia in the lowest time will win the hunt.
